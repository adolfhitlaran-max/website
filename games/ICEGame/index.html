Rules of Engagement

Your team is collecting evidence off-screen. Each wave lasts 2 minutes. When the timer hits zero, spawns stop — clear remaining hostiles to move forward.

Foot Runners Use the Tazer to stun and stop them.
Getaway Vehicles Use the Gun to disable vehicles. (Tazer won’t work.)
Score Rules Vehicle takedowns score higher. Using gun on foot = score penalty.
Support Units Drone tazes foot only. Humanoid robot handles both.
